Link-4 game is a two-player game. Players need to take turns to click boxes on the board. Player1 has red moves, while player2 has blue moves. The player who makes 4 continuous moves horizontally, vertically or diagonally wins the game. Both players' scores will be displayed on the top. A new game can be created when the "New Game" button is clicked. Players are also allowed to save their scores to a txt file named "scores.txt". To save or show the score of a player, the player must provide username and password. A new player's username and password will be created when the player saves score for the first time.
